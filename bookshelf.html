SS#5: Somewhere In Neverland

asereneko

Epistolary

When life offers no refuge and the weight of reality feels unbearable, 18-year-old Emory finds an unexpected escape. After a chance encounter with a mysterious boy claiming to be Peter Pan, she’s swept away to Neverland—a world untouched by time, where adventure and magic thrive. But Neverland isn’t the innocent paradise she once imagined. Shadows lurk, secrets whisper through the forest, and Peter himself carries a haunting burden.

As Emory unravels the mysteries of this enchanted realm, she discovers that finding "somewhere" may mean confronting her deepest fears and learning that even in a place of eternal youth, growth is the only way forward.

"If there's nowhere on Earth, maybe there's somewhere in Neverland" is a tale of hope, self-discovery, and the courage it takes to rewrite your own story.

143: I Hate You

MajKtisis

novelette

A thrilling adventure awaits in *The Lost City*. When a team of archaeologists uncovers a long-forgotten map leading to a hidden city, they embark on a journey fraught with danger and excitement. The map is rumored to hold the key to an ancient civilization's secrets, and the team is determined to unlock them.

As they venture deeper into uncharted jungles and treacherous mountains, they face natural obstacles, wild animals, and the eerie feeling that someone—or something—is following them. The mysteries surrounding the city grow more complex as they uncover clues to its existence. But not all is as it seems, and the line between friend and foe becomes increasingly blurred.

With each discovery, the team members must question their motives and the true purpose of their expedition. Will they uncover the secrets of the Lost City, or will the forces that guard it prove too powerful to overcome?
